Project 2 - Historical Stock Backtester

Test real stocks against real market history.

Enter a stock symbol, choose a time window, and compare trading strategies against actual historical price movement. The app fetches market data, runs a browser-side backtest, and shows trades, drawdowns, and performance against buy and hold.

What Changed

This version uses historical stock data instead of synthetic Monte Carlo paths.

It is still educational and should not be treated as financial advice. Strategy signals are simplified and execute on the bar close inside the selected range.

Important note

This app fetches historical prices from Alpha Vantage in the browser. Standard keys usually provide enough data for short daily tests and much longer weekly or monthly tests.
